Audira

An assistant aimed to help patients and the therapist

Between therapy sessions, patients can open up with Audira’s human-like voice assistant anytime they need. It listens, transcribes, and summarizes the conversation, sharing key insights with the therapist, helping them stay informed and making the most of their time during actual sessions.

Human-Like Voice Conversation

Every day there are new advancements in AI, and many of these are open source and available to use and improve by everyone. We believe that with what is already public a conversational AI that can show understanding and human-like empathy is possible.
Audira's AI will introduce itself, explaining what it does and how it can help patients reflect, explore their emotions, and help their therapist's job.

Reporting Between Sessions

Each conversation is transcribed, allowing specialists to review the patient’s real, unfiltered expressions, especially during critical moments. By capturing interactions beyond weekly appointments, Audira can help detect mood swings that might otherwise go unnoticed. Every session also includes a summary with suggested topics for the next therapy session.”

Secure Monitoring

Through a secure access point, therapists can retrieve patient data, monitor progress and usage, and manage each patient’s access to the conversational AI. All data is end-to-end encrypted, ensuring that only the therapist and the patient can access their conversations.

Many Possibilities

More features are not excluded! Personalized care for each patient with different fine-tuned models, booking management for appointments, mood tracking, progress charting, online consultancy...

Business Model

Audira runs on a subscription model, with a fee per patient using the service. Anyone can try our conversational AI, recommend it to their therapist, or find nearby specialists already using Audira.
